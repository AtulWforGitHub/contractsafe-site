Step-by-Step Guide
How to Analyze Any Contract
in 5 Simple Steps

No legal knowledge needed. ContractSafe does the hard work — you just review the results and decide.

1

Select Your Contract Type

Choose from Employment, Rent / Lease, Sale Deed, Loan, NDA, Vendor, or Freelance. This tells ContractSafe what to focus on — rent agreements get different analysis than employment contracts.

💼 Employment 🏠 Rent 🏡 Sale Deed 💰 Loan 🔒 NDA
2

Upload or Paste Your Document

Upload a PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT file — or take a photo of a printed contract and upload as JPG/PNG. AI vision reads scanned documents automatically. Or simply paste the contract text directly.

📌 Tip: Upload the complete document. ContractSafe reads every page — the most important clauses are often buried on the last pages.

3

Click "Analyze Contract"

ContractSafe reads the entire document and checks every clause against Indian standard practice — Indian Contract Act, Labour Law, RERA, IT Act, Stamp Act. Results arrive in about 30 seconds.

4

Review Your 10-Section Report

You get a structured report with: plain English summary, risk table with ₹ impact, benefits, obligations, important clauses, missing protections, negotiation points, Indian legal compliance, and a numbered action checklist.

📌 Tip: Click any row in the risk table to simulate "What happens if I trigger this clause?" — including financial exposure.

5

Take Action Before Signing

Use the built-in tools: Negotiation Email (one click drafts a professional letter), Clause Rewriter (get a fair alternative for any risky clause), Follow-up Chat (ask specific questions about your contract), or Export PDF/Word to share with your lawyer.
